Porridge

Home

A simple recipe to making porridge from scratch in under 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 40g Oats
  • 200ml Milk
  • 2 Teapsoons Sugar

Steps

  1. Add oats and milk to a bowl and mix well.
  2. Microwave for 1 min then remove and stir well.
  3. Continue to microwaves for 30 second intervals then removing and stiring until desired thickness has been reached.
  4. Finally, stir in the sugar.
