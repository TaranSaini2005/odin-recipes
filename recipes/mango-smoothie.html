Mango Smoothie

Home

A quick and easy recipe for a refreshing mango smoothie!

Ingredients

  • 50g Greek Yogurt
  • 150ml Semi-Skimmed Milk
  • 80g Frozen Mango
  • 1 Medium Banana
  • 2 Tablespoons Porridge Oats
  • 4 Tablespoons Water
  • 1 Teaspoon Honey

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into blender. Blend until smooth.
